Offroad Trails

There are a bunch of trails around the state but here are a few to give you a few ideas for your trip.

Trail Name Description Location Length Difficulty
Toquerville Falls Toquerville, UT Rocky at the start before smooting into a dirt road. You will face numerous river crossings on the way back. 11.8 mi Moderate
Moab Rim Trail Moab, UT Popular trail with stunning views, it is dangerous and not for stock vehicles or inexperienced drivers. It is a shared trail with hikers as well so if you don't have a car feel free to hike it! 8 mi Hard
Skyline Drive Farmington, UT A little rocky at times but nothing that can't be handled by a stock vehicle. It can also be hiked. Forest scenery and you can see out over Farmington/Bountiful at the top. 23.9 mi Easy
Kyhv Peak Road Provo, UT Popular trail for camping, cross-country, skiing, and horeback riding. Has some potential wash out areas so be wary of those. 24.3 mi Moderate
Temple of the Sun and Temple of the Moon Hanksville, UT A rough dirt road that is bumpy and has some up and down terrain but driveable by most vehicles. 34.3 mi Easy
