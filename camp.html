Campsites

This page will have some campsites that your family and friends can go and spend the weekend soaking in the scenery!

Campsite Name Description Location
Jug Hollow Dutch John, UT Easy trail to get to the camp and very spacious camping spots. Not a lot of shade so its best to go later in the year when it's not as hot
Valley of the Gods Mexican Hat, UT There are several car camps along this 17-mile loop, any car should be able to naviagte this trail no problem.
Kolob Resevoir Cedar City, UT This mountain resevoir is a popular place for the desert dwellers of St. George. It has toilets and a boat dock and is restricted to snowmobiles only in the winter.
Goblin Valley Green River, UT Other than having a cool name, this campsite is great for those looking for seclusion and peace. It's a bit sandy and the road is a little rough getting in to the flat spots and natural rock walls.
Willow Spring Heber City, UT Getting here is tought but oonce you do you'll find secluded campsites and some natural beauty. Bring bug spray since ticks are know to be in the area.
